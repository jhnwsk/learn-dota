# Learn Dota 2 - Beginner's Tutorial

Welcome to the Dota 2 beginner's tutorial! This guide will help you understand the basics of one of the most complex and rewarding competitive games ever made.

## About This Tutorial

This tutorial is designed for complete beginners who have never played Dota 2 or MOBAs before. We'll cover everything from the basic concepts to your first game, with a special focus on **Hoodwink** - a fun and beginner-friendly hero.

## How to Use This Tutorial

1. **Read in order** - Start with "What is Dota 2?" and work your way through
2. **Don't memorize everything** - Focus on understanding concepts, not memorizing details
3. **Practice as you learn** - Try playing bot matches to apply what you learn
4. **Take your time** - Dota 2 has a steep learning curve, and that's okay!

## Why Hoodwink?

Hoodwink is an excellent hero for beginners because:
- Her abilities are straightforward to understand
- She's ranged, making positioning easier
- She's fun to play with satisfying skill shots
- She can be effective even when learning

Good luck, and have fun learning Dota 2!
